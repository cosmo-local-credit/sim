# Agent Defaults

- This repository is the public Cosmo-Local Credit simulator.
- Simulator changes should follow the Regenerative Bonds paper mechanisms while still emphasizing emergent cosmo-local credit.
- Keep the simulator scientific: expose assumptions, record failure modes, and prefer falsifiable metrics over promotional claims.
- Full empirical data from `RegenBonds/cleaned_data/` is local and must not be committed here.
- The hosted public simulator may later use small derived calibration artifacts only after dedicated simulator analysis and planning.
- Do not edit `protocol` or `eth-tracker` from simulator work.
- Preserve reproducibility through explicit parameters, seeds, scenario presets, and documented calibration paths.
